JLPT


  • The Japanese-Language Proficiency Test (日本語能力試験, Nihongo Nōryoku Shiken), or JLPT, is a standardized criterion-referenced test to evaluate and certify Japanese language proficiency for non-native speakers.
  • Based on levels it is categorized into 5 levels i.e N5 from most basic to N1 which is the most advanced level.
  • 80/180 Marks are needed to pass JLPT N5 level.
  • JLPT consists of 3 sections i.e Language knowledge , Reading , Listening (60 marks each).
  • Total time for the test is 90 mins from December 2020.
