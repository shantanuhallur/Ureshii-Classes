Features

Why choose URESHII Classes ?

Well Designed Curriculum

We thrive to provide you with learning content that is more relatable and easy to understand and always back you up with more and more examples of the topic with doubt solving sessions followed afterwards.

Cultivating Interest

Language learning is easy when you have something about the language you are attached to. Thats why a lot of focus is put into cultivating intrest of the candidate rather that just sticking to the JLPT portion.

Test Series of 5 Papers

We ensure that the candidate gets used to the test environment as well as build confidence for their final evaluation.

Native Guest Appearance

Learning language without having any idea of native pronunciations and language flow can be difficult. To resolve this we invite a Native Japanese Guest so that students can really get a feel of the language.
